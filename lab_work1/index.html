Manga - The Fascinating World of Japanese Comics

What is Manga?

Manga refers to Japanese comic books and graphic novels. It has a long and rich history and is enjoyed by readers of all ages around the world.

Popular Manga Genres

  • Shōnen (Action/Adventure)
  • Shōjo (Romance/Drama)
  • Seinen (Mature themes)
  • Josei (Women-centric)
  • Kodomo (Children's manga)

Famous Manga Series

Title Author Genre
Naruto Masashi Kishimoto Shōnen
